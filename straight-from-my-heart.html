"Straight From My Heart: Life's Experiences Through Poetic Expression" is a compilation of poems divided into six parts. In each part the author expresses emotions and thoughts many people experience while on their journey through life. Whether you are journeying through faith, parenting, grief, falling in love, resisting love, getting hurt by those we trust with our hearts, realizing how society treats others or getting to know the true essence of self, this book of poetry touches the spirit.
